## Problem

**Why most beginner Python courses fail to keep children engaged.**

- Passive video lessons teach watching, not coding.
- Abstract examples without a story lose attention fast.
- Mistakes feel like failure instead of part of learning.
- Parents want their child to learn real skills, not just click through gamified fluff.
- Text-heavy explanations assume adult reading stamina.

---

## Solution

**Python Quest is different.**

- Every Python concept is part of a steampunk adventure with characters who guide, question, and celebrate your progress.
- Students write **real Python code** from the very first mission — not blocks, not drag-and-drop, real syntax.
- Mistakes are met with friendly hints from the characters, not red crosses or score penalties.
- Progress is visible: quest maps, skill unlocks, completion markers.
- By the end of beta, your child understands variables, conditions, loops, functions, and data structures — and can write small Python scripts independently.

---

## How It Works

1. **Get beta code** — Purchase or receive your beta access code. No account setup, no email, no password.
2. **Start quest** — Enter the code, one click, and the story begins. No install, no PATH config, no setup.
3. **Learn through story and analogy** — Ва explains Python concepts using everyday things: variables as labelled chests, conditions as forks in the road.
4. **Complete missions** — Write real Python code. The Mission Checker gives instant, friendly feedback. Новичок celebrates. Багус helps when things go wrong.
5. **Continue with restored progress** — Switch devices? Clear your browser? Enter your beta code again and pick up exactly where you left off.

## Who It Is For

## Who It Is Not For

- **Advanced Python developers** — this course covers fundamentals only.
- **Learners who prefer only video or textbook learning** — Python Quest is interactive and narrative-driven.
- **Anyone looking for a finished, mass-market product** — this is a **commercial beta**, actively being improved through feedback.

---

## Beta Limitations

- **This is a beta.** The product is validated and stable, but not mass-market polished. Some wording, pacing, and UI details may still be rough.
- **No parent dashboard yet.** Progress is visible inside the student's session, but parents cannot log in separately to view progress.
- **No full account system.** Access is managed via beta codes, not email/password logins.
- **12-week access period.** Beta access is time-limited. Post-beta terms will be communicated separately.
- **Payment is manual.** You will receive a payment link — there is no automated in-product payment flow yet.
