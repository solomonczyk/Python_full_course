## How To Start

1. **Open the link:** [https://python-quest.vercel.app/](https://python-quest.vercel.app/)
2. **Enter your beta code:** Enter the code in the beta access field
3. **Start Part 1:** Click "Start" and begin the adventure
4. **Complete first lessons:** Help Ada, Chen, and Bagus on the space station
5. **Submit feedback:** After Part 1, fill out the feedback form

> ⏱ Part 1 takes approximately 20–40 minutes depending on reading pace.

## What Is Staged Access

Python Quest has 3 parts, but only Part 1 is open when you start. Here's why:

- **Part 1** — opens immediately with your beta code
- **Part 2** — locked until you share feedback on Part 1
- **Part 3** — locked until you share feedback on Part 2

This is intentional. Staged access helps me:
- Focus your child on one part at a time
- Catch and fix issues before more content opens
- Make sure each part works well before you see the next one

## What Happens After Feedback

1. You submit the feedback form
2. The operator reviews it (usually within 24–48 hours)
3. If everything looks good, Part 2 is unlocked
4. You receive a notification
5. Your child continues the adventure

> ⚠️ Part 2 unlock is not automatic. It requires your feedback and operator review.

## What To Do If Something Breaks

If something doesn't work:

1. **Refresh the page** — most issues resolve with a refresh
2. **Check the browser** — use latest Chrome or Firefox
3. **Take a screenshot** (if possible) and send it to me
4. **Describe the problem** — what were you doing, what happened, what did you expect?
5. **Message me directly** — I'll help as soon as I can

> 📱 Contact: [operator contact info]
>
> ⏱ Response time: usually within a few hours during working hours

**Common issues:**
- "Nothing happens when I click Start" → Try refreshing or clearing browser cache
- "The code checker says I'm wrong" → Check for typos, case sensitivity, and punctuation
- "A lesson looks strange" → Take a screenshot and send it to me
- "I lost my progress" → Make sure cookies/local storage is not cleared between sessions

**Emergency / critical issues:**
If the course is completely broken (blank page, crash on start, etc.), message me immediately so I can fix it for everyone.
